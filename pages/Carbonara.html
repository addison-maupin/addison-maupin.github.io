Mama Maupin's Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • ½ lb pancetta
  • 3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup freshly grated romano cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese
  • 2 tbsp. chopped parsley
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Spaghetti

Preparation:

  1. Cook sphagetti in generously salted water and reserve some water for the sauce.
  2. While pasta is cooking, add pancetta to a cast iron and cook until the edges just begin to crisp (can add oil if necessary). Turn off heat.
  3. Mix eggs, cheese, pepper, and parsley in a serving bowl until smooth. To make the carbonara extra creamy, you can add 1 cup pasta water to this mixture as well.
  4. Drain the pasta by transferring the spaghetti with tongs to to the cast iron.
  5. Add the egg mixture to the pan (no heat!!!) and toss quickly.


Addi's Notes:

Perhaps my mother's most treasured recipe, I still struggle to make this. I often cook the eggs on accident. Still delicious but definitely be careful with temperatures and mix the pasta as quickly as possible.
