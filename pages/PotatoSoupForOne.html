Potato Soup for One

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 medium (250g) Russet potato, peeled and cubed
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ¼ tsp. pepper
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp. flour
  • Cheese
  • Green onions
  • Bacon bits

Preparation:

  1. In a large pot, saute onions and garlic until tender.
  2. Add broth, potatoes, sal, and pepper and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, stir milk and flour together until smooth.
  5. Slowly pour the milk mixture into the soup and bring to a boil until thickened, about 2 minutes.
  6. Top with bacon, cheese, and green onions.


Addi's Notes:

It is very sad having to make potato soup for one person but this recipe is fast and easy! Makes two servings and would be easy to double if you want more. For more flavour, cook your own bacon in the pan and then saute the garlic and onion. I normally never have bacon, so I just top with green onions and cheese.
