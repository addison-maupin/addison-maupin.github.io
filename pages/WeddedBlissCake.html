Happy Marriage Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ cup loosely packed brown sugar
  • ½ tsp. baking poweder
  • ¼ tsp. cinnamon
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup rhubarb jam

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Mix all the ingredients except the jam in bowl. The mixture should be crumbly but soft.
  3. Reserve ¼ of the mixture for the topping and put the rest of the mixture into a 7-inch tart pan.
  4. Press the mixture firmly into the pan and spread the jam evenly over the base.
  5. Crumble the topping over the base.
  6. Bake 25 minutes or until a dark golden brown.


Addi's Notes:

Ate this cake many different times in Iceland. It was delicious every time!
