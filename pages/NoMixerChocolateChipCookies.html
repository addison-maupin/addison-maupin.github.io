No Mixer Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled to room temperature
  • 1 ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 ½ cup flour
  • 1 ½ tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Whisk floud, salt, and baking soda together and set aside.
  3. Whisk brown and white sugar and melted butter for 1 minute until light.
  4. Mix in one egg at a time and add vanilla.
  5. Add in the dry ingredients and chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop out dough and refrigerate while oven preheats.
  7. Bake for 10-14 minutes or until golden brown and top with flaky sea salt.


Addi's Notes:

Quick and easy cookies when there is no mixer available. They are very thin and crunchy.

Presentation: 8/10

Taste: 7/10
