Polish Mama's Potato Salad

Ingredients:

  • Small red/golden potatos
  • 1 thinly sliced leek or onion
  • 1 handfull chopped dill
  • 1 handfull chopped gherkins
  • 1 thinkly sliced celery
  • 2 tbsp. mayo
  • 1 tbsp. mustard
  • 3 tbsp. plain greek yogurt
  • salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

  1. Chop the potatoes to bite size chunk and add to boiling water. Cook until tender.
  2. Add leek, dill, gherkins, and celery.
  3. Mix with mayo, mustard, yogurt, and add salt and pepper.


Addi's Notes:

Another dish that is easy to make for a bunch of people! We got this recipe from our polish friends in the UK and I like it more than the mustard based potato salad I grew up with.

Presentation: 5/10

Taste: 8/10
