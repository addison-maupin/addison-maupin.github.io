Mama Maupin's Buttermilk Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • &frac13 cup butter
  • 2 cups self-rising flour
  • ¾ cups buttermilk (usually needs a bit more)

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Cut butter into flour with a pastry blender or two knives until just crumbly.
  3. Add buttermilk, stirring until dry ingredients are just moistened. Be gentle or your biscuits will be tough.
  4. Pat onto a lightly floured surface and barely kneed 3-4 times.
  5. Pat dough to ¾ inches think, cut out biscuits, and place in cast iron skillet.
  6. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes.


Addi's Notes:

A childhood staple. Always reminds me of home.
