Pistachio Christmas Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 box yellow cake mix
  • 1 box instant pistachio pudding mix
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup oil
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 cup sour cream

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. In a smaller bowl mix ¼ cup sugar and 1 tsp. cinammon.
  4. Add mixture to 2 bread loaf pans and press the cinammon sugar topping firmly into both loaves.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until done. Allow to cool slightly then transfer to wire rack.


Addi's Notes:

One of Mom's student made this for her one year. I thought it was healthy until I realized it was just yellow cake. Delicious and easy and it makes two loaves, perfect for sharing or freezing.

Presentation: 10/10

Taste: 10/10
