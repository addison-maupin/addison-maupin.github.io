No Mixer M&M Cookies

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ¾ cup flour
  • ½ tsp. baking soda
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • 1 ½ cup M&Ms

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Microwave the butter for 40 seconds until completely melted, but not hot.
  3. Mix butter and sugars.
  4. Mix in vanilla and egg.
  5. Add flour, baking soda, and salt and mix until combined.
  6. Stir in M&Ms
  7. Scoop out cookies and bake for 7-10 minutes.


Addi's Notes:

Quick and easy cookies when there is no mixer available. You can also substitute chocolate chips for M&Ms but I prefer the M&M cookies. For chocolate chip cookies I prefer this recipe

Presentation: 3/10

Taste: 6/10
