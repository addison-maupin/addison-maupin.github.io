Caramelized Leek Chicken with Lemon Parmesan Orzo

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 leek
  • Garlic
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • White wine
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup orzo
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Pound chicken to even thickness and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil and butter in skillet over medium-high heat and sear chicken for 2 minutes on each side. Set aside when done.
  4. Turn heat down and caramelize lemon slices in same pan and set aside when done.
  5. Add leeks to skillet with a little more butter, splash of apple cider vinegar, and salt and pepper and let leeks caramelize.
  6. Add chopped garlic and orzo to the pan and toast.
  7. Deglaze with white wine and add stock.
  8. Add chicken back to the pan and top with lemon slices.
  9. Cook for 15 min.
  10. Top with Italian parsley and shaved parmesan.


Addi's Notes:

Easy week night meal that makes about 3 servings. Its definitely better fresh out of the oven, but the leftovers stay nice, especially with extra parmesan :)

Presentation: 4/10

Taste: 7/10
