Arugula Salad with Fruit, Chevre, and Pomegranate

Ingredients:

  • Peaches or Pears (depending on season)
  • 3 tbsp. white balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp. Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp. honey
  • 4 tbsp. EVOO
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper
  • Arugula
  • Goat's cheese
  • pomegranent seeds

Preparation:

  1. If peaches are in season, cut each peach into 4 - 6 wedges and preheat a grill pan to high heat. Brush each side of fruit with olive oil then grill each side for about 2 minutes. Use a spatula to flip (not tongs!).
  2. If peaches are not in season, use pears. Thinly slice pears and set aside.
  3. Add the vinegar, mustard, and honey to a small bowl and whisk to combine. Slowly add the olive oil while whisking to emulsify. Season to taste.
  4. In a large bowl, add arugula and toss with &frac13 of dressing. Top with fruit, cheese, and pomegranate seeds. Drizzle with remaining dressing.
  5. When ready to eat add avocado, crushed tortilla chips, and ranch.


Addi's Notes:

Quick tasty salad that makes a good side. I learned this recipe from a cooking class, he served this alongside chicken piccata. This is a farily light salad, so it definitely needs to be served alongside something else to be filling.

Presentation: 8/10

Taste: 8/10
