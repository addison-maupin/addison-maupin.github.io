Mama Maupin's Pound Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb softened butter
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 6 large eggs, room temp.
  • 4 cups flour
  • ¾ cup milk, room temp
  • 1 tsp. almond extract
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 300F.
  2. Beat butter until creamy and lighter. Gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time and beating just until the yolks disappear.
  4. Add flour alternately with milk, beginning and ending with flour. Beat at a low speed until just incorporated then add in almond extract and vanilla.
  5. Pour into a greased and floured 10inch tube pan and bake for 1:40.
  6. Rest in pan for 10 minutes and then remove to a wire rack to cool.


Addi's Notes:

Classic cake, the crunchy parts are the best! Pound cake can be a little dry (especially if you want the crunchy bits) so you can serve this with berries and whipped cream to add some moisture.

Presentation: 10/10

Taste: 10/10
