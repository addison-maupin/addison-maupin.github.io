Marcella Hazen's Tomato Sauce III

Ingredients:

  • 28oz Pomodoro tomatoes/li>
  • 5 tbsp. butter
  • 1 yellow onion
  • Salt
  • ¼ tsp. sugar

Preparation:

  1. Add tomatos, butter, onion, salt, and sugar to a pan and cook at a low simmer for 45 minutes.
  2. Mash any large chunks of tomato with a fork or wooden spoon.


Addi's Notes:

This is a treasured recipe and so easy to make. I always have a jar of tomatoes on hand to make this. I have found that this sauce will last pretty well in the fridge although not as long as store-bought sauce. I sometimes add a clove or two of garlic along with the onion. Always top with parmesan and preferribly basil if on hand!

Taste: 10/10
