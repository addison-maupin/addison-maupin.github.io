Trader Joe's Brussel Sprout Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza crust
  • 1 bag shaved Brussel Sprouts
  • Garlic spread dip
  • Quatro Formaggio Cheese
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Pepper

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 450F.
  2. Spread garlic dip on pizza crust.
  3. Mix brussel sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top pizza with brussel sprouts and cheese.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and pepper.


Addi's Notes:

Perfect meal for a Friday night! Fun to assemble and you still get a bit of veggies! I heard this was even better if you top with Trader Joe's Spicy Honey :)
