Cold Rice Noodle Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce, thinly chopped
  • Purple cabbage, shredded
  • Cucumber, shredded
  • Carrots, shredded
  • Red Pepper, thinly sliced
  • Edamame
  • Rice Noodles
  • 2 tbsp. peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. siracha
  • 1 tsp. honey
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil

Preparation:

  1. Cook rice noodles and set aside
  2. Combine peanut butter, soy sauce, siracha, honey, and olive oil in a jar and set aside.
  3. Add lettuce, veggies, and rice noodles to a bowl and top with dressing.


Addi's Notes:

Fun and colorful salad! I made this with 2 heads romaine lettuce, 1/2 head cabbage, 1/2 cucumber, and a whole red pepper and this lasted me for 5 meals! Kept well in the fridge and the peanut sauce is delicious!

Presentation: 7/10

Taste: 8/10
