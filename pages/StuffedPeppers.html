Turkey Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 3 bell peppers, sliced in half with seeds removed
  • ½ lb ground turkey
  • ½ yellow onion, diced
  • Oregano
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Tomato sauce
  • ½ cup rice, cooked
  • Cheese
  • Parsley

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375F
  2. Brown the turkey in skillet then add onion, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Cook until onion is tender.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Stir in brown rice and remove from heat.
  4. Stuff the peppers and bake uncovered for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Top peppers with cheese and bake another 10 minutes. Garnish with parsley.


Addi's Notes:

Fun meal but it took a while to prepare. In future, I would do less rice and serve with more veggies for a better balance.

Presentation: 8/10

Taste: 5/10
