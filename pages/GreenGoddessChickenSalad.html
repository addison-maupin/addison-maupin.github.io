Green Goddess Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • ½ red onion, diced
  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • 3 chicken breasts, cooked and diced
  • ⅓ cup walnuts
  • ⅓ cup pumpkin seeds
  • ⅓ cup mayo
  • ⅓ cup yogurt
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 1 cup cilantro

Preparation:

  1. Toast walnuts and pumpkin seeds in pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, lettuce, chicken breast, and nuts to a bowl.
  3. Add mayo, yogurt, lime juice, green onions, parsley, cilantro, and salt and pepper to a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Add dressing to the bowl and mix until incorporated.


Addi's Notes:

Takes a bit of time to prepare since you have to cook the chicken and make a dressing but it is very good. I use whatever lettuce I have on hand and add avocado as a little treat. I normally make 3 servings in advance and it keeps quite well although the chicken starts to taste a little too chickeny by the last serving.

Presentation: 6/10

Taste: 7/10
