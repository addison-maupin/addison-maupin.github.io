Parmesan and Leek Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • 2 medium leeks, halved and sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken stock
  • parsley, chopped
  • 1 ¾ cup parmesan, shredded

Preparation:

  1. Melt butter in a pot or frying pan.
  2. Add leeks and cook for 10 minutes until softened, then add garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice until coated in mixture then add white wine and allow to bubble for a minute.
  4. Add the stock a cup at a time, simmering and stirring as you go for about 20 minutes. If you run out of stock you can use boiled water.
  5. Rice should be cooked and creamy but still have a bite.
  6. When ready to serve, stir in cheese and sprinkle with parsley.


Addi's Notes:

A classic recipe from my mama so it's delicious every time. You can easily leave out the wine, Marcella Hazen's recipe for Parmesan Risotto skips the wine and just adds a bit of stock.
