BBQ Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • BBQ sauce
  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • Fire roasted corn
  • Cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avacado
  • Crushed tortilla chips

Preparation:

  1. Saut√© chickpeas in BBQ sauce until they are sticky.
  2. Shred Romaine lettuce.
  3. Add halved cherry tomatoes, fire roasted or steamed corn, cilantro, red onion, and BBQ chickpeas.
  4. When ready to eat add avocado, crushed tortilla chips, and ranch.


Addi's Notes:

Easy lunch to make for work and one of the only ways I will eat beans! I add whatever lettuce I have on hand and cheese as a little treat. Fire roasted corn is the best but any canned or frozen corn works. I normally make three servings in advance and they save quite well!
