Salmon Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 Salmon filet
  • Chili powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Brown rice, cooked
  • Cucumbers, sliced
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Avacado
  • Jalepeno
  • Mayo
  • Siracha
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Lime

Preparation:

  1. Start by cooking rice if needed.
  2. Cut salmon into cubes and toss with chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add salmon to hot skillet with oil and sear on all sides until crispy.
  4. Chop all veggies.
  5. In a small bowl, mix mayo, siracha, soy sauce, and honey together and set aside.
  6. Add rice to a bowl, top with salmon and veggies, drizzle with the spicy mayo sauce and finish with a squeeze of lime.


Addi's Notes:

Easy meal and fun way to eat salmon! I mix my rice with cilantro and add lettuce for extra veggies. I don't really have any measurements for the spices, any amount works, it's hard to mess up!

Presentation: 9/10

Taste: 8/10
