Sauce Basics

Notes:

  1. Heat pan to appropriate temperature.
  2. Add fat and heat.
    • Butter - low heat, heated when its melted and foaming subsides
    • Olive oil - moderate heat, heated when shimmery
    • Canola oil - neutral moderate heat
    • Grapeseed oil/Safflower oil - neutral high heat
  3. Add protein and cook until done.
    • Chicken
    • Fish fillet
    • Steak
    • Tofu
  4. Remove protein and set aside.
  5. Add vegetables and heat enough fat to cook.
    • Mushrooms
    • Onions
    • Peppers
    • Zucchini
  6. Lower heat and add more fat. Add aromatics and saute until soft, not brown.
    • Shallots
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
    • Combination
  7. Add deglazing liquid and swirl and scrape the brown bit (frond) off the pan.
    • Wine - simmer, do not boil
    • Spirits
    • Juice
  8. Add flavoured liquid and reduce to ½ or &frac13 depending on how much sauce is needed.
    • Wine - reduce at a simmer
    • Stock - reduce at a boil
    • Vinegar
  9. Add flavorings and heat through
    • Vinegar
    • Mustard
    • Shoyu
    • Balsamic
    • Capers
    • Lemon juice + zest
    • Tomato paste
  10. Add finishing ingredient to the sauce.
    • Unsalted butter, cold
    • Cream
  11. Add protein and reheat both sides.
    • Plate with starch and veg of choice.
    • Place a little sauce on the top of the protein.
