Sauce Basics

Notes:

  1. Heat pan to appropriate temperature.
  2. Add fat and heat.
  3. Butter - low heat, heated when its melted and foaming subsides
  4. Olive oil - moderate heat, heated when shimmery
  5. Canola oil - neutral moderate heat
  6. Grapeseed oil/Safflower oil - neutral high heat
  7. Add protein and cook until done.
  8. Chicken
  9. Fish fillet
  10. Steak
  11. Tofu
  12. Remove protein and set aside.
  13. Add vegetables and heat enough fat to cook.
  14. Mushrooms
  15. Onions
  16. Peppers
  17. Zucchini
  18. Lower heat and add more fat. Add aromatics and saute until soft, not brown.
  19. Shallots
  20. Garlic
  21. Ginger
  22. Combination
  23. Add deglazing liquid and swirl and scrape the brown bit (frond) off the pan.
  24. Wine - simmer, do not boil
  25. Spirits
  26. Juice
  27. Add flavoured liquid and reduce to ½ or &frac13 depending on how much sauce is needed.
  28. Wine - reduce at a simmer
  29. Stock - reduce at a boil
  30. Vinegar
  31. Add flavorings and heat through
  32. Vinegar
  33. Mustard
  34. Shoyu
  35. Balsamic
  36. Capers
  37. Lemon juice + zest
  38. Tomato paste
  39. Add finishing ingredient to the sauce.
  40. Unsalted butter, cold
  41. Cream
  42. Add protein and reheat both sides.
  43. Plate with starch and veg of choice.
  44. Place a little sauce on the top of the protein.
