Aunt Michelle's Black Bean and Corn Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans black beans
  • 2 cans corn
  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 1 small diced red onion
  • 1 diced fresh jalepeno (with or without seeds)
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1-2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 2 diced avacados
  • salt and pepper to taste (light on pepper)

Preparation:

  1. Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate.
  2. Serve with tortilla chips
  3. Note: if making in advance, add the tomato and avacado right before serving and taste as it sometimes needs more lime or salt after sitting in the fridge.


Addi's Notes:

Easy to make for a bunch of people! I don't love beans so I normally do 1 can beans and 2 cans corn. I like to make it in advance as the flavor is a bit stronger. It also keeps well for a couple of days so you can use leftovers for salads etc.

Presentation: 7/10

Taste: 8/10
