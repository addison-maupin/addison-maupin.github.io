Peasant Bread Focaccia

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 2 tsp. active dry yeast

Preparation:

  1. Whisk the flour, salt, and yeast in a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in the water and add to the flour mixture and mix.
  3. Dough will be sticky, but cover and set in warm place to rise for 1 to 2 hours.
  4. Preheat oven to 425 and spray baking pan.
  5. Pat and pull dough to fit in pan. Drizzle olive oil onto dough and continue to spread to fit pan.
  6. Create dimples in dough and add rosemary and sea salt.
  7. Allow to rise 20 to 30 minutes.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes at 425 then lower to 375 and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.


Addi's Notes:

Mom always make us this with the Parmesan and Leek Risotto. It is so good I could eat the whole pan. If you want to make peasant bread instead of focaccia, divide the dough into greased pyrex bowls (1.5 L or 1.5 qt but not bigger) after the first rise. Allow to rise for 30 minutes then bake at 425 for 15 minutes and 375 for another 15 minutes.
