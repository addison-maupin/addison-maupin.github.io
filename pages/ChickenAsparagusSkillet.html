Chicken and Asparagus Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken breasts, cubed and seasoned
  • 1 bunch Asparagus
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 tsp. garlic
  • 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp. parley, minced
  • 1 tsp. cornstarch
  • Rice
  • Lettuce

Preparation:

  1. Blanch asparagus for 2 minutes.
  2. Cookin chicken on medium heat until golden brown. Lower the temperature, add garlic, and cook until fragrant. Set chicken aside.
  3. In same pan, add remaining garlic and deglaze with chicken broth. Bring to a simmer and add butter, lemon juice, and parsley.
  4. Add asparagus and stir until covered then add chicken and reheat.


Addi's Notes:

I use vinegar or wine instead of chicken broth normally. I also only use about 1tbsp butter for the sauce normally.

Presentation: 7/10

Taste: 6/10
