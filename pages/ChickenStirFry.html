Easy Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast, cubed and seasoned
  • Carrots
  • Frozen broccoli
  • Garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tsp. honey
  • 2 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1 tsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tsp. cornstarch
  • Rice
  • Lettuce

Preparation:

  1. Cook chicken and set aside.
  2. Cook carrots and broccoli until tender.
  3. While cooking veggies, combine water, honey, sesame oil, soy sauce, and cornstarch in a small bowl.
  4. Add garlic and chicken to skillet and cook for a minute.
  5. Add sauce and cook until thickened.
  6. Serve with rice, lettuce, and other veggies of your choosing.


Addi's Notes:

Super easy meal, I always keep chicken and broccoli in the freezer for this.

Presentation: 6/10

Taste: 7/10
