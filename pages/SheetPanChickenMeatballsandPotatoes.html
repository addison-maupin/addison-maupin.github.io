Sheet Pan Chicken Meatballs and Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs Baby potatoes, quartered
  • 2 oz. Feta
  • 1 tbls. Parsly, minced
  • 1 tbls. Mint, minced
  • 1 tsp. Greek seasoning
  • Oregano
  • 1/2 Red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup Breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbls. Lemon juice + zest
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 lb. Ground Chicken

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 450F.
  2. Coat potatoes in olive oil and season with Greek seasoning and oregano.
  3. Roast potatoes for 20 minutes.
  4. Add feta, parsley, mint, Greek seasoning, red onion, and breadcrumbs to a pan and mix lightly
  5. Add egg and ground chicken and mix with hands until evenly incorporated
  6. Divide mixture into 16 50g meatballs.
  7. Push potatoes to one side and drizzle Â½ pan with olive oil, add meatballs, and cook for another 15 minutes.
  8. Finish by broiling for 5-10 min or until crispy


Addi's Notes:

Easy week night meal that makes about 4 servings. I normally do not have Greek seasoning so just use oregano. The first time I made this, I replaced the potatoes with roasted zucchini but the meal was not very filling, so next time I will do a mix of roast potatoes and veggies. I also like to serve this on a bed of lettuce with tzatziki, parsley, and a little lemon.
