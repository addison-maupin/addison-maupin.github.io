Zabaglione with Seasonal Fruit

Ingredients:

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp. sugar
  • ¼ cup Prosecco
  • &frac18 tsp. kosher salt
  • ½ tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cup seasonal berries

Preparation:

  1. Whisk together all the ingredients except the fruit to form a custard base.
  2. Over a double boiler (best to use glass) heat the custard mixture and whisk continuously until thermometer reads 175F. Custard should be thick enough to hold a ribbon when a whisk is passed through it.
  3. Pour into ramekins and refrigerate until ready to serve. Top with fruit.


Addi's Notes:

Learned this recipe in a cooking class. It was really easy but not the most delicious. It is weirdly frothy, just tastes like bubbles.

Presentation: 8/10
