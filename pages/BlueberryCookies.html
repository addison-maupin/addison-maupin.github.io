Blueberry Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ tsp. baking powder
  • &frac18 tsp. salt
  • &frac13 cup butter
  • &frac13 cup and 1 tbls. sugar
  • &frac13 cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup white chocolate chips

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Thaw the blueberries in the microwave for 30 seconds, until soft and jammy.
  4. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, then add the blueberries and cream at high speed until the blueberries are pureed and fully combined with the butter mixture.
  5. Fold in the dry ingredients and add the white chocolate chips.
  6. Chill dough for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Bake for 10-13 minutes or until slightly brown around the edges.


Addi's Notes:

Fun recipe because they are so purple and taste like a little blueberry muffin! I chilled my dough for 30 minutes the first time and it was far too long.

Presentation: 10/10

Taste: 10/10
